About me

Welcome to my portfolio website! My name is Josh York, and I'm a passionate full-stack web developer with a strong affinity for science, technology, psychology, space and astronomy, camping, photography, web design, computer programming, and ketogenic health and biochemistry.

I firmly believe that programming and technology have the power to transform our world for the better. One of my driving motivations is to utilize these tools to create connections and foster inclusion for individuals with autism or those who are neurodivergent. I envision a future where technology acts as a bridge, bringing together people from diverse backgrounds and abilities, fostering understanding, and providing support to those who need it most. Through my portfolio, I aim to showcase my skills in web development, design, and programming, while also expressing my deep-rooted desire to contribute positively to the world. I invite you to explore my projects and learn more about how I hope to make a difference through my work.

Thank you for visiting, and I look forward to connecting with you!
